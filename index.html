Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare

Artificial intelligence (AI) is transforming healthcare by improving diagnosis, personalizing treatment, and streamlining administrative tasks.

Applications of AI

  • Medical Imaging: AI helps detect diseases from X-rays, MRIs, and CT scans with high accuracy.
  • Predictive Analytics: AI can predict patient outcomes and help in early intervention.
  • Virtual Health Assistants: AI-powered chatbots provide patients with guidance and support.
  • Drug Discovery: AI accelerates the discovery of new medications and therapies.

AI in healthcare not only enhances efficiency but also improves patient care and decision-making for healthcare professionals.
